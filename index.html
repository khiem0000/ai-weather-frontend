Planner Guide

1. View Weather: Click on any date in the calendar to see the forecast for that specific day.

2. Add Tasks: Type a task in the "Add new task..." box and press ENTER to save it.

3. Manage Tasks: Check off completed tasks. Hover over a task to Edit or Delete it.

4. Smart Alerts: The bell icon will display a red dot if you have tasks scheduled for tomorrow.

5. Quick Navigation: Click 'Home' to view the yearly calendar and jump directly to any month.

Chart Guide

Air Quality Index (AQI)

The spider chart visualizes harmful pollutants in the air:

  • PM2.5 & PM10: Fine particulate matter.
  • NO2, CO, SO2: Factory and vehicle emissions.

💡 Tip: The wider the inner shape spreads, the more polluted the air is.

Sun Path & Astro

  • The glowing dot represents the real-time sun position based on the location's timezone.
